A NEW PROMPT

It is a period of rapid prototyping.

Rebel engineers, striking
from hidden branches,
have achieved their first victory
against vague requirements
and unstable demos.

During the battle,
the team managed to steal
enough GPU time to prepare
a presentation about models,
agents, automation,
and things that may or may not
have worked five minutes ago.

Pursued by deadlines,
the speakers race toward launch,
determined to deliver a talk
capable of inspiring colleagues
throughout the company...
